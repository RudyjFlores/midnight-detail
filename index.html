Common Questions

Do you bring your own water and supplies? +
Yes — we bring everything. Our van is fully stocked with water, a pressure washer, generator, and all products. You don't need to provide anything or be near a hose.
Do I need to be home while you detail my car? +
No. As long as we can access the vehicle and you've confirmed the booking details, you can be at work, inside, or anywhere. We'll text you when we're done.
How long does a detail take? +
Maintenance Clean: 1–1.5 hours. Full Detail: 2–3 hours. Add-ons add roughly 30–60 minutes each. Our two-person team means it's done faster than a solo detailer without cutting corners.
What areas do you cover? +
We serve all of Broward County — Fort Lauderdale, Hollywood, Pembroke Pines, Miramar, Coral Springs, Davie, Plantation, Sunrise, Deerfield Beach, and surrounding areas. Not sure if we cover you? Text us.
How do I pay? +
We accept card, Cash App, Zelle, and cash. Payment is collected at the time of service.
What if I need to reschedule? +
No problem. Just text us at least 24 hours before your appointment and we'll get you rescheduled. We understand life happens.
